# 🚀 Election App - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to Jan-Mat India!

Your complete gamified learning platform for understanding Indian elections with achievements, progress tracking, and interactive quizzes.

---

## 📱 Getting Started

### 1. **Open the App**
   - Open `index.html` in your web browser
   - You'll see the home screen with 6 learning sections

### 2. **Your Learning Dashboard**
   - **Progress Bar**: Shows how many sections you've completed
   - **Motivation Message**: Changes as you learn
   - **Achievement Badges**: Visual indicators of your progress
   - **Navigation Cards**: Click any card to start learning

## 🏆 Achievement System

### Four Achievements to Unlock:

| Badge | Name | Requirement | Icon |
|-------|------|-------------|------|
| 🌱 | **Getting Started** | Visit any section | Seedling |
| 🧭 | **Explorer** | Visit 3+ sections | Compass |
| 🏅 | **Election Learner** | Visit all 6 sections | Award |
| 👑 | **Quiz Master** | Score 80%+ on quiz | Crown |

### How Badges Work:
- Locked badges appear gray and inactive
- Earned badges glow in saffron color with box-shadow effect
- Hover over earned badges to see their name
- Badges unlock automatically when conditions are met

## 📊 Progress Tracking

### What's Tracked:
- **Sections Visited**: Monitored automatically
- **Quiz Score**: Highest score is remembered
- **Achievements**: All unlocked badges recorded
- **Progress Percentage**: Calculated as (Visited Sections / 6) × 100%

### Progress Display:
- Main progress bar at top of home screen
- Completed sections show green checkmark ✓
- Dynamic motivation messages
- Percentage display (0-100%)

---

## 🧠 Quiz System

### Quiz Structure:
- **5 Questions** covering key election concepts
- **Multiple Choice**: A, B, C options
- **Topic Tags**: Shows what each question covers
- **Progress Indicator**: Shows your position (1/5, 2/5, etc.)

### Scoring:
- 1 point per correct answer
- Scale: 0-5 points
- Displayed as: Score/5 and Percentage

### Quiz Feedback Levels:

#### Perfect Score (100% - 5/5) ⭐⭐⭐
- Title: "🏆 Quiz Master!"
- Message: Perfect mastery achieved
- Recommendation: Explore advanced scenarios

#### Excellent (80-99% - 4/5) ⭐⭐
- Title: "⭐ Excellent Work!"
- Message: Strong grasp of elections
- Recommendations: Suggested topics to review

#### Good (60-79% - 3/5) ⭐
- Title: "👍 Good Job!"
- Message: On the right track
- Recommendations: Specific sections to study

#### Learning (40-59% - 2/5)
- Title: "💡 Keep Learning!"
- Message: Making progress
- Recommendations: Core sections to review
- Links: Suggested overview/timeline content

#### Beginner (0-39% - 0-1/5)
- Title: "🚀 Let's Learn Together!"
- Message: Starting the journey
- Recommendations: Overview section focus

## 💾 Data Storage

### How Progress is Saved:
- **Storage Method**: Browser localStorage
- **Storage Key**: `janMatState`
- **Persistence**: Survives page refreshes
- **Scope**: Device and browser-specific

### What's Saved:
- Visited sections list
- Unlocked achievements
- Highest quiz score

### Clear Your Progress:
To reset and start over:
1. Open Browser DevTools (F12)
2. Go to Application → LocalStorage
3. Find and delete `janMatState` entry
4. Refresh the page

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Will my progress be saved?**  
A: Yes! All progress is saved in your browser's localStorage and persists across sessions.

**Q: Can I retake the quiz?**  
A: Yes! Click "Try Again" after completing a quiz. Your highest score is tracked.

**Q: How do I unlock the Quiz Master badge?**  
A: Answer 4 or more questions correctly (80%+ score) on the 5-question quiz.

**Q: What if I close the browser?**  
A: Your progress is saved and will load when you reopen the app (if you don't clear browser data).

**Q: Can I access this on mobile?**  
A: Yes! The app is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets.

**Q: How do I reset my progress?**  
A: See "Clear Your Progress" section above, or your can manually reset via browser DevTools.
